Acquisition criteria

What fits our model.

A few signals we screen for early. They are guidelines, not hard rules, and good firms that fall outside them are still worth a conversation.

Firm type
Fractional finance, accounting, bookkeeping, tax, and FP&A practices.
Revenue
Roughly $1M to $10M in annual revenue.
Profitability
Profitable today, with healthy and durable margins.
Recurring revenue
A high share of retainer or recurring client work.
Clients
A diversified, long-tenured client base with low concentration.
Team
A stable team and leaders who want to keep building after a deal.
Geography
North America, ideally near our hubs in San Francisco, Toronto, and Montréal. Open to remote-first teams.
