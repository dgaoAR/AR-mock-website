About

A different kind of owner for finance firms.

Alloy River is a holding company built to be a permanent home for finance and accounting firms — an alternative to selling to a roll-up that cuts the firm for parts.

The best finance firms should outlast their founders, not be sold for parts.

Why we exist

Good firms deserve better than the usual options.

Stuck on aging software

Many firms still piece together a stack of old systems that don't talk to each other. Work gets duplicated, errors creep in, and good people spend their days on busywork instead of clients.

Sold for parts

When a founder is ready for a new chapter, the usual buyer is a roll-up that cuts costs, folds the brand, and moves on. We think a firm worth building is worth keeping.
