TEAM PHOTOS
===========

Drop each team member's photo into this folder, then open team.html and
edit the TEAM list near the bottom of the file:

  {name:"Jane Doe", title:"Co-Founder & CEO", photo:"jane-doe.jpg", bio:""}

- "photo" is just the filename here (e.g. jane-doe.jpg).
- Portrait images work best (roughly 3:4, e.g. 600 x 800px).
- Leave photo:"" to show the person's initials instead.
